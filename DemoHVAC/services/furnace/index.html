Practical furnace guidance for Florida homeowners.

How often should a furnace be inspected?+

A fall inspection is a smart baseline, especially before the first cold night. It checks ignition, airflow, electrical connections, and venting.

When should I replace my furnace?+

Age, safety, repair history, comfort, and efficiency should all be considered. We show both paths side by side.

What if I smell gas?+

Leave the home and call 911 or your utility first. Do not operate electrical switches or re-enter until it is safe.
