What is the best way to improve indoor air?+

Start with the filter and airflow, then look at humidity, duct condition, ventilation, and the needs of the people in the home.

Can HVAC control humidity?+

Yes. Proper sizing, coil performance, controls, and dedicated dehumidification can all improve comfort.

Do air cleaners replace filters?+

They complement filtration. We design the combination around equipment capacity and the needs of the home.
