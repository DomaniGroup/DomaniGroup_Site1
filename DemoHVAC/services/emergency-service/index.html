A calm guide for urgent HVAC situations.

What counts as an HVAC emergency?+

No heating or cooling in extreme conditions, smoke or electrical smells, active water leaks, and situations that could damage the home should be treated urgently.

Should I turn the system off?+

If there is smoke, a burning smell, active water, or unusual electrical behavior, turn the system off safely and call.

Can I track the technician?+

Yes. We send appointment updates and a live ETA once a technician is assigned.
