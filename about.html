Joseph Sysko — Founder & CEO

Joseph Sysko is the founder and CEO of Domani Group, where he leads a team focused on turning complex federal data into decisive mission and business advantages. A results-oriented leader with a blend of military precision and private-sector agility, he specializes in data analytics, business intelligence, digital transformation, and decision-support systems for government clients.

His career spans high-stakes financial management and advanced analytics for the U.S. Air Force, the National Guard Bureau (overseeing multi-billion-dollar portfolios), and top-tier consulting engagements supporting senior leaders.

Throughout, he has built and led cross-functional teams that deliver mission-critical insights, streamline operations, and drive measurable, documented outcomes.

At Domani Group, Joe remains hands-on, partnering with executives, operators, and analysts to harness the full power of their data—enabling smarter decisions, greater efficiency, and a durable competitive edge.
